I'm a Software Engineer and like building stuff.

Studied philosophy as an undergrad. Best decision ever. However, employers didn't agree about its usefulnes, so I studied some finance and started working at a hospital. So, why not go back to school, get an advanced degree from a stellar university, and have a blast learning the newest developments (Artificial Intelligence, anyone?).
