Projects (Not Listed on GitHub)

837 Parser

837 files contain patient claim transaction data from a healthcare organization. Few alternatives exist to parse the complicated formating of these files. Using Python and Regular Expressions these files are parsed and a simple, easy to read output is produced. While working at a previous employer this parser saved my employer a monthly subscription to a external vendor who provides such functionality.

Charge Capture Assessment Tool

Consultants often spend several days to weeks assessing clients prior to offering services or implementing solutions. This tool automateis repetitive tasks associated with a Charge Capture Assessment. The tool tested data integrity, performed descriptive statistical analysis, and modeled potential future states.
